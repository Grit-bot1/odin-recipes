Cajun Sweet potatoes

Home

Sweet and salty and Spicy

Healthy snack, or delicious side dish!

Ingredients

  • Spices(cajun)
  • Sweet potato, about 3lbs
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Gather and scrub the sweet potatoes
  2. Cut them to your preferred size
  3. Add them to a large stainless steel bowl and add oil
  4. Toss with salt and spices until covered
  5. Spread onto lightly oiled pan and put into preheated(425c) oven for 35-65 minutes depending on the size you decided to cut them
