Succulent brined turkey breast!

Very easy to make, only 3 ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Turkey
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Brine the turkey for 4-12 hours in salt water
  2. After finished, pat dry skin
  3. Season heavily with salt and pepper all over
  4. Preheat a heavy bottomed pan with oil until rippling
  5. Put turkey in pan skin side down and do not touch until the edges of the skin are visibly golden brown
  6. Flip the turkey and put into a preheated 400 degree(celsius) oven for about 20 minutes, or until thermometer shows 160c
  7. DO NOT SKIPRest the turkey for at least 10 minutes before cutting.
