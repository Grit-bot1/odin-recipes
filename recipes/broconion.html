Broccoli and Onions

Quick and simple side

Ingredients

  • Broccoli
  • Onion
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cut onion into 4, slice broccoli to preferred size
  2. Toss with olive oil and season with salt and pepper
  3. Lay on preheated sheet pan and put into 400(c) oven for 20 minutes or until broccoli is charred.
